ParticleRenderer.velocityScale 速度大小

var velocityScale : float

Description描述

How much are the particles strectched depending on "how fast they move"

粒子被拉伸多少取决于"它们运动多快"。

Use this to make particles get longer as their speed increases.

用这个使粒子随着它们的速度增加而变长。
