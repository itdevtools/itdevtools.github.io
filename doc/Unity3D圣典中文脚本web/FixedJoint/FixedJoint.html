FixedJoint 刚性连接

Inherits from Joint

The Fixed joint groups together 2 rigidbodies, making them stick together in their bound position.

刚性连接2个刚体群组在一起，使它们的边界位置粘在一起。

See Also: CharacterJoint, HingeJoint, SpringJoint.
